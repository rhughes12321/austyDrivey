About me

I am Happy to answer any question's you may have, my number is 07917808206. I have been a fully qualified Approved Driving Instructor ( ADI ), since 2004. My objective us to teach you to drive safely and pass your test as quickly as possible. Lessons are structured to suit your confidence and ability. More than 85% of my pupils pass first time, usually taking between 20 to 30 lessons. I offer discounts for intensive courses, block bookings and frequent lessons. I also offer you a free driving lesson when you refer a friend or other family member to me. I am always punctual and reliable, I won't let you down. I am pleased to liaise with any parents or other family member who may be assisting you with your private practicing. I am married with 3 children, before I became a driving instructor I was a HR Manager, I am passionate about my job and take immense satisfaction from seeing former pupil's out driving on their own.
